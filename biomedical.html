Department of Biomedical Engineering

Discover the intersection of biology and technology through our Biomedical Engineering program. Students learn to solve real-world medical problems with innovative engineering solutions.

Degrees & Focus Areas

  • Bachelor of Science in Biomedical Engineering – core curriculum in biology, engineering, and medical device design.
  • Research Projects – work with faculty on health-related engineering solutions.

Faculty & Labs

Learn from experienced researchers in biomedical instrumentation, biomechanics, and biomaterials. Access hands-on lab experiences that prepare you for grad school or industry roles.

Student Involvement

  • Join the Biomedical Engineering Society (BMES)
  • Participate in innovation competitions
  • Shadow professionals through our health partnership programs
